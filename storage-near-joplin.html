Getting Here from Joplin

Head west on US-400 toward Parsons, or take US-59 north. The drive is about 50 minutes and our facility is right on US-59 with easy highway access. Gate access is available from 6am to 10pm, seven days a week.

Storage Unit Sizes Available

We have a range of unit sizes to fit your needs, from compact 10x10 units for a bedroom's worth of items to large 20x30 units that can hold the contents of a full house. All units are insulated, have drive-up access, and include a free lock. View our full size guide to find the right fit.

Month-to-Month Leases

No long-term contracts. All our units are rented month-to-month, so you only pay for the time you need. Give us 10 days notice and move out without being charged for additional months.
