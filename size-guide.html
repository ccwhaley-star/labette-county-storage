Storage Tips

How to Choose the Right Size

Make a List

Write down everything you plan to store. It helps to sort items by size — furniture, boxes, appliances, and oddly shaped items.

Think Vertical

Stack boxes and lighter items on top of heavier furniture. Using the full height of your unit means you may need a smaller size.

When in Doubt, Go Bigger

A little extra room makes it easier to organize, access your belongings, and add items later. Plus you won't feel cramped.
